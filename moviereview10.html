.......Movie Review..........

Title:Hridayam

Cast:Pranav Mohanlal,KalyaniPriyadarshan and Dharshana Rajendran

Director:Vineeth Sreenivasan

Music by: Hesham Abdul Wahab

Rating:4.5/5.0

Highlights:

Hridayam offers its version of a fun campus life, and a chance for those who miss it, to reminisce about their youthful days. Arun and Darshana are first-year engineering students and they fall in love soon after their college begins. And just as how things work generally in teenage, the road ahead isn't too easy for them. Life offers many twists and turns that they least expect. There are many hearty songs in the film and they are all well-placed.Mohanlal lends an undeniable charm to it, making Hridayam a memorable watch. At a time in which college students hardly get to experience their campus or form friendships, Hridayam offers its version of a fun campus life, and a chance for those who miss it, to reminisce about their youthful days.
